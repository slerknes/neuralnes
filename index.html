About

I am a postdoc using scalp and intracranial EEG to investigate how we process and integrate environmental information, from how representations of external stimuli are altered by brain state to how we established shared representations during communication.

Research

  • Sleep and coma
  • Auditory Processing
  • Hyperscanning
  • Scalp EEG
  • Intracranial EEG
